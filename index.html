The Life of Matthew Hardy

Summary

Hello, Welcome to the short version of my life. I grew up in Middletown, PA. I went to school at Middletown elementry, middle, & highschool. I started my first job at a Cafe at the age of 15 and from there saved up enough money to buy my first car at the age of 18. I worked at the Cafe for 3 years and moved on to a variety of different jobs after that throughout the years which includes demolision landscaping, construction , warehouse, lottery, investment, ecommerce and sales jobs.

I have been working in sales most of my life and enjoy the process of making stuff to sell on various sites or sites i built. I am working on teaching myself new skills that I can apply to future roles to help me be proficient and understand a variety of different jobs. I keep my My Linkedin upto date if you would like to follow my journey on there. I try to take every chance I can to learn something new! I am currently enrolled in Correlation-One software classes to help me understand how to design, build and deploy websites on my own. So far I learned HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

My goal is to design and build a creative website that looks good and can capture and hold the attention of the targeted audience. Where I can create revenue from sales and advertising to help relieve some finacial pressure without having to pay an arm and a leg for fees to use other peoples websites. Another goal of mine is to learn how to build scripts for stock charts to help me get signals for reading the market, so i can confidently re-invest my money without losing it all. Along with sales I really enjoy the thrill of trading stocks and options. It keeps me upto date on current events and helps me stay in the loop on macroeconomics.
